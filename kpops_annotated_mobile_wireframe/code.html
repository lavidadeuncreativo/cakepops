Annotated Wireframe

Mobile-First High-Conversion Landing Page for Kpops Pachuca.

1

Sticky Header: Quick social access

Logo + Instagram/Map shortcuts. Ensures credibility and location verification is always 1 tap away.

2

Hero: 1-screen decision info

Concise value proposition: Price ($), Locality (Pachuca), and Speed (15-30m). Everything needed to decide is above the fold.

3

Quick Picks: Lateral scroll

Visual categories for rapid item selection. Reduces scrolling friction and improves discoverability of best-sellers.

4

Sticky Bar: 70/30 CTA split

Major action (70%) is "Order Now" to Instagram DM. Minor action (30%) is "Full Menu" to avoid choice paralysis.

5

Modals: Light & Focused

Secondary info like "Opening Hours" or "Flavor Details" trigger as modals. Keeps the landing page short and lightning-fast.

info Conversion Logic

This wireframe prioritizes the Mobile Decision Journey. By limiting vertical depth and focusing on the CTA split, we drive users directly to the high-converting Instagram DM interface.
